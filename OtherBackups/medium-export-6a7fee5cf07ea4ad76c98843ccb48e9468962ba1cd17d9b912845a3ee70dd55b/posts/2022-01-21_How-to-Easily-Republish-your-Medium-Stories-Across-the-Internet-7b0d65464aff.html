How to Easily Republish your Medium Stories Across the Internet

Cross-posting can give you writing even more traction and IFTTT makes it simple to do

Over the past few weeks, I’ve started writing across several platforms. Writing in multiple places has been helping me grow my audience, but taking the time to copy the content to each site is annoying.

Thankfully, Medium has a nice import story feature. However, I just discovered a better way of quickly republishing posts via IFTTT. IFTTT is an acronym standing for if this then that. It is a website that allows you to create applets that execute in the cloud.

Applet creation is a low-code feature that allows complete customization and integration with multiple platforms. IFTTT’s explore page describes the creation process as

  1. Choose your trigger
  2. Choose your action
  3. Name your Applet and hit Finish

For example, I’ve been experimenting with posting stories to my blogger page. IFTTT made the process of taking my stories and reposting them to blogger automatic. Whenever I hit publish on Medium a repost creation is trigger!

If you’re interested in saving time and growing your writing cross-platform then I would highly recommend checking out IFTTT.
