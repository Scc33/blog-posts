I’ve Joined the Medium Partnership Program and You Can Too

Consistency and a little bit of hard work will get you there

Right before hitting the publish button on an article for the first time a few things crossed my mind:

  • Attempting something new is daunting!
  • Getting 100 followers is a big scary number!
  • Committing to a public goal like writing is terrifying!
  • How can I commit to writing?

With all those fears in my mind, I almost gave up right away. However, it’s okay to be afraid to put yourself out there. I’ve found Medium to be a welcoming place where people try to help each other.

Over the past few months, I’ve grown as a writer and become more confident in expressing my thoughts. Now I’ve joined the Partnership Program which feels like a milestone to me.

If you are just starting out like I was only a few weeks ago then I have two pieces of advice.

  • Take things one step at a time by focusing on just that one article you’re writing.
  • Stay consistent and good things will come.

Best of luck! You can do this!
