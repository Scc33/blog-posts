How to Track Medium Statistics

Gain a better grasp of your reach with new tools

I’ve previously written about using statistical tools to track my Medium writing. As I’ve grown on Medium, my conviction in the importance of these tools has grown too.

Good tools to use

Medium Enhanced Stats is the most popular option. It offers a popup feature and additional data on the statistics page. Check out my previous review of Medium Enhanced Stats to learn more.

Medium Enhanced Stats
Your total reach & summary of your articles and responses stats with a single click and many improvements of the Medium…chrome.google.com

Another tool to use is Better Medium Stats. Better Medium Stats offers helpful graphs to show follower growth, views over time, and story stat comparisons.

Better Medium Stats
View your Medium stats in a better way and export data to csv file.chrome.google.com

I love charts, so the plethora of options with Better Medium Stats has me practically giddy.

I have been using Better Medium Stats to visualize my story performance over various timeframes.

The tool has also been useful to see how I’m reaching and growing my audience.

Use the best tools at your disposal

I am a believer in using every tool available to me. I would recommend trying out both of these applications. They are helpful motivators, offer good insights, and are fun!
