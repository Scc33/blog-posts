How to Set Reasonable Medium Goals

A Journey to reaching sustainability with writing on Medium

I started writing on Medium in early January 2022. Despite the ironic timing, I have gone back and forth on calling it a New Year’s resolution.

I wanted to grow my online presence and get my thoughts out into the world. I figured writing would help clear my head and coherently express my thoughts. Ambitiously, I wanted to become thinker, employee, friend, and partner.

I set off at an ambitious pace. I quickly published an article or two a day and almost burnt myself out. After so much rapid writing, I slowed down and set more measured goals. I’ve settled into a routine of one story a week.

Many writers on Medium proclaim writing a story a day. I think that pace is just too fast for the average person. We all live busy lives with other responsibilities. Trying to scoop too much onto your plate is a recipe for burnout.

I’d recommend discerning what you want to achieve and setting smaller goals to get there. Committing to writing an article a day is hard, but deciding to write just one is easy.

Perhaps the best recommendation I could give is to ignore me. Find what works for you. But keep writing and keep chasing your goals. Just try to do it sustainably.

By Sean Coughlin on February 18, 2022.
