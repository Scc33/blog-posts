Grinding to 100 Followers

I want YOU to get to the magical 100 followers too. Let’s do this together.

After joining Medium I quickly realized that my first goal would be to hit 100 followers. Medium runs a Partner Program to help writers on the platform earn money. The partner program requires users to have 100 followers to apply.

I know many of you might be fighting for 100 followers. So I’ll make this pledge to you reader. If you follow me, I’ll follow you back.

I’m planning on blogging mostly about technologies and software development. Occasionally I might throw in something more personal. My goal is to make every piece I write useful, engaging, and insightful.

I also want to grow with the community. I think the writers on Medium have so much to offer and I want to learn from their experiences.

Let’s do this together! Follow me and I’ll help you get to that special 100 follower mark. Best of luck and keep on writing.

By Sean Coughlin on January 18, 2022.
