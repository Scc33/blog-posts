Try Out Medium Enhanced Stats

Gain a better understanding of your reach with this amazing Chrome extension

Medium offers a decent, albeit limited, overview of your writing statistics. By default, Medium makes it easy to track views, reads, and fans. One of the best writing tools I’ve discovered is Medium Enhanced Stats. It takes the statistics overview offered by Medium and bumps it up to the next level.

I’m a fan of numbers, and data has always excited me. One way I motivate myself is by checking my statistics. Since starting to use the Enhanced Stats tool, I’ve been more motivated to write and share my thoughts with others.

The tool works with Chrome and is super easy to install. It offers a popup feature and additional data on the statistics page.

The popup feature shows an overview of your Medium Stats and additional info like the Clap to Fans ratio. It has a goal-setting feature and will create milestones for you to hit.

Medium Enhanced Stats adds more detail to the view, reads, and fan charts by highlighting days with published stories.

It adds more detail to the individual story statistics like current day metrics and the number of claps.

The tool has helped motivate me to become a more consistent writer. I would recommend that new and experienced writers check it out. I hope it can do the same for you.
