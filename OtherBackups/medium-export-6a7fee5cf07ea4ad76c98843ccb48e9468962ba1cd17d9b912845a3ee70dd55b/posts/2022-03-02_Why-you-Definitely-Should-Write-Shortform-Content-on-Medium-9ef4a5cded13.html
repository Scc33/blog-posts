Why you Definitely Should Write Shortform Content on Medium

Shortform is a quick way to write more great content on Medium

Just like it sounds, shortform is a very tiny article. Medium considers an article to be shortform when it 150 words or less. If an article falls under that threshold then Medium displays it to users differently. The key benefit of shortform is the ease. Writing just 150 words only takes a few minutes.

Pros

  • Shortform is easy and quick to write!
  • Not every writing idea is very meaty and some of the best ideas lend them selves to being short and sweet.
  • The entire post appears in profile and publication feeds on Medium so readers have a high probability of seeing your content.

Cons

  • Shortform is not eligible for additional distribution from the Medium curators.
  • Shorter articles will make less money from the partnership program.
