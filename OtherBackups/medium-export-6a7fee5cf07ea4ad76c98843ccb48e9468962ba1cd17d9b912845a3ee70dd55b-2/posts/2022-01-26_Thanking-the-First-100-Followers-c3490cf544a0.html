Thank you to everyone who has followed my journey.

Nine days, eleven stories, and fifty-six responses later the community has bestowed me with the magical 100 followers mark. I want to say thank you!

Reaching 100 followers has taken pressure off and allowed me to focus on creating more great content. Stay tuned for stories about software engineering and technology.

I’ll still follow you back if you follow me! I think that follow for follow is a great way to grow the community and it helps keep my Medium feed interesting :).
