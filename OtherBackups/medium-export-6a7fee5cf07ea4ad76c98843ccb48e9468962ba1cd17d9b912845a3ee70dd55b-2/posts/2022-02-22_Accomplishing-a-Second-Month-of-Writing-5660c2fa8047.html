Sharing my stats

I think accountability is key to staying motivated. One way of staying accountable is through working in the public eye. In order to show my progress publicly and stay motivated, I’m sharing monthly updates of my writing stats.

Additionally, I would have liked to see stats when I started writing. So hopefully I am encouraging new writers by showing them my journey.

Stats on the Second Month

In my second month of writing, I have gained 152 new followers, 508 reads, and 1,474 claps bringing my total views to 1,208 and 727 reads!

I saw growth in most areas with noticeable declines in fans per read and claps per view. Initially, most of my posts were classic follow-for-follows where readers had an incentive to engage because it benefited their own growth. Now that I’ve shifted my content, I need to focus on driving engagement through the merits of my content.

Insights

In my first post, I wrote: “I didn’t imagine how positive the response would be and how much traction I could get with a little effort.” I don’t think that is accurate anymore.

Writing is a tremendous amount of work and it is mentally exhausting. However, staying consistent and putting yourself out there can start something special. I still think it’s worth it, but it’s more difficult than I anticipated.

Stay tuned for another month of writing!
