There are few truer words than time flies. I cannot believe that I’m three months into my writing journey. When I started writing, I vowed to find consistency. I have been achieving consistency through staying accountable. One of my methods of staying accountable is by sharing my statistics.

Statistics

For in depth Medium statistics tracking, I use Medium Enhanced Stats. I follow a number of metrics to capture the size of my audience as well as how engaged they are.

Insights

I would call this another month of steady growth. I haven't had one story blow up or “a big break.” However, consistency has lead to strong month over month growth that I’m pleased with. For this month I have three new takeaways.

  1. I started my journey by adhering to the follow for follow method. This generated a lot of fans and followers. Some of this growth caused artificially high engagement numbers.
  2. I rolled many of my stories over to the partnership program this month to start generating income. This caused a moderate drop in readership of those stories as they went behind the paywall.
  3. Stories that are a few months old still generate steady readership and my growing back catalog is important to raising viewing numbers.

I hope these insights help your own writing and stay tuned for another month!
