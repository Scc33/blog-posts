One Month of Writing

If I can do it, you can do it too.

I’m not sure if I can call it a New Year’s Resolution, but I started writing in January of 2022. I wanted to learn to express myself and get thoughts out in public. Medium has been a friendly community to share and grow within.

I’m a big fan of numbers and find myself motivated by tracking my statistics. The stats offered on Medium have been a gold mine and tremendously motivating for me.

In my first month of writing, I have accumulated 160 followers, 219 reads, and 1,481 claps across 13 stories. The screenshot above includes responses while the screenshot below only includes data on stories I’ve written.

I’m sharing my statistics because it keeps me honest, and I think it could encourage other new writers. I didn’t imagine how positive the response would be and how much traction I could get with a little effort.

If you are just getting started, I want to encourage you to take that step. Publish that article you’re working on, respond to that author you like, follow for follow other authors. All these techniques work and make you a better writer. When combined with consistency, putting yourself out there can start something special. I wish my very best to new writers.

I’m planning on sharing monthly updates of my stats as a way of staying honest and committed to writing.

By Sean Coughlin on February 2, 2022.
